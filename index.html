Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make Sugar Syrup: Boil sugar and water, add cardamom & saffron. Simmer for 5-7 mins. Turn off the heat & add rose water.
  2. Prepare Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee. Add warm milk gradually to form a soft dough. Rest for 10 mins.
  3. Shape Balls: Grease hands, make smooth small balls (no cracks).
  4. Fry Jamuns: Heat oil/ghee on low-medium flame. Fry balls until golden brown.
